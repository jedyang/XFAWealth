Investment Goal

Over the past few months, we have taken individual stock decisions that have further reduced the portfolio’s exposure in financials companies (active in insurance sales and stock market operations). We have also slightly increased exposure to companies with a higher share of sales outside of Europe, in markets such as Asia and Latin America, where we see economic recovery potential.

Recommended reason

We expect equity markets to remain unsettled over the coming weeks, although news from individual companies will help clarify which are the relative winners and losers, particularly as the first half results season will start in three weeks’ time. More broadly, the referendum outcome will likely result in reduced risk appetite and also in lower forecasts for economic growth in Europe and the UK.

Investor Suitability

The key areas we are reviewing for Brexit risk are primarily centred on holdings with exposure to the UK consumer. In particular, we are reviewing holdings which might be affected by a short-term fall in consumer confidence.
