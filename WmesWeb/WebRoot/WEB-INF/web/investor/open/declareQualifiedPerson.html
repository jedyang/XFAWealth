Only applicable if a qualified person (other than the Advisor) certifies copies of the HKID^/Passport of the applicant(s) and the signing of the client agreement. I (i.e. the Qualified Person), the undersigned, hereby confirm that I have identified the applicant(s) who executed this Application Form before me, and that I have reviewed the original of his/her/their HKID(s)^ or passport(s).
